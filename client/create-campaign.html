📋 Before You Begin

Platform Eligibility Rules & Policies

Please read all 10 rules carefully. You must accept them before creating a campaign. All rules are automatically enforced by our AI and HMS system.

🏥 Medical & Identity Verification Rules

Enforced automatically — no exceptions

1
🪪
One Active Campaign per Patient Aadhaar
No two campaigns can be active simultaneously for the same patient Aadhaar number. A new campaign can only begin after the previous one is fully closed, completed, or rejected.
🤖 Auto-checked
2
💰
Annual Household Income Must Be ≤ ₹3,00,000
Verified from the government-issued Income Certificate you upload. If the AI extracts an income above ₹3 lakhs per year, the campaign is automatically rejected — no exceptions.
⚡ Strict limit
3
✅
Patient Name Must Match Across All 3 Sources
The patient's name as written on the Aadhaar Card, Ration Card, and Hospital Management System database must be identical. Even minor differences trigger a flag for manual review.
🧠 AI cross-checks
4
🏥
Hospital Admission Must Be Confirmed Live by HMS
The patient must currently be admitted, have treatment pending, or be in active recovery. This is confirmed in real-time through the Hospital Management System. Self-reported medical details are never accepted.
🤖 Live HMS check
5
🎯
Campaign Goal = HMS Outstanding Amount — Not Self-Declared
The fundraising target is pulled directly from the HMS billing system as the outstanding payable amount. You cannot set or change this figure — it is set automatically after HMS confirmation.
⚡ Cannot be changed

📎 Document & Process Rules

AI reads and verifies all documents you upload

6
📄
All 5 Documents Are Mandatory — No Exceptions
You must upload: Aadhaar Card, Ration Card, Income Certificate, Hospital Estimate, and Admission/Discharge Summary. Missing or unreadable documents will block the campaign from proceeding.
⚡ All 5 required
7
🤖
AI Extracts All Medical Details — You Do Not Fill Them
Hospital name, pincode, diagnosis, admission date, and cost estimates are extracted automatically by AI from your uploaded documents. You only confirm the extracted data — there is no medical details form to fill manually.
🧠 Fully automated
8
✍️
Campaign Story Must Pass AI Review Before Going Live
Your story is reviewed by AI for factual consistency with HMS and document data. Stories that contradict verified medical details, appear misleading, or are factually inconsistent will be flagged before the campaign goes live.
🧠 AI reviewed
9
👤
Fundraiser Must Truthfully Declare Relationship to Patient
You must accurately declare whether you are the patient, a family member, a friend, an NGO representative, or hospital staff. False declarations are grounds for immediate campaign removal and account suspension.
⚡ Legal declaration
10
🏦
Funds Released Directly to Hospital — Never to Individuals
All donated funds are disbursed in tranches directly to the verified hospital's bank account, based on treatment milestones confirmed by HMS. No cash is released to the patient, fundraiser, family member, or any individual at any point.
⚡ No individual payouts
